5. Notes to Accounts (Summary)

Significant accounting policies applied in the preparation of these financial statements:

1. Basis of Preparation

Prepared in accordance with "Regulations Governing the Preparation of Financial Reports by Securities Issuers" and IFRS endorsed by the FSC of Taiwan.

2. Revenue Recognition

Revenue is recognized when control of products transfers. Measured at fair value of consideration received/receivable, net of returns/discounts.

3. Inventories

Stated at lower of cost (weighted-average) or net realizable value. Provisions made for devaluation when NRV is lower than cost.

4. Property, Plant & Equipment

Stated at cost less accumulated depreciation. Straight-line depreciation over useful lives (Buildings: 10-50y, Machinery: 3-10y).

5. Provisions

Provisions (e.g., warranty) are recognized when there is a present obligation from past events and probable outflow of resources.

6. Foreign Currency

Functional currency is NT$. Transactions translated using exchange rates prevailing at transaction dates.
